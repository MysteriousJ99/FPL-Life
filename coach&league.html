Enzo Maresca is an Italian professional football manager and former player. He is the head coach of Premier League club Chelsea. After starting out at West Bromwich Albion in 1998, he went on to play for several clubs in his country, including Juventus and Fiorentina. He retired in 2017.

Serie A, Italy's top soccer league, was founded in 1929 as a national round-robin competition. Its golden era came in the 1980s and 1990s with clubs like Juventus, AC Milan, and Inter dominating Europe. Today it remains a cornerstone of world football.
